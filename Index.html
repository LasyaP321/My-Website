My Favorite Books

One of my favorite mystery series of all time is Nancy Drew. The stories are excitingly thrilling, and capture the reader's attention within the first few sentences.

Geronimo Stilton is written in 2 seperate points of view: Geronimo and Thea Stilton. There are a few more, but these 2 are the most widely read. These are the perfect books for when you need a short bedtime story or when you want to read a mini-novel.

A - Z Mysteries are centered around 3 elementary students solving mini crimes. A haunted hotel? Sure! A missing author? Why not? You name it, A - Z Mysteries have it!

Great Places to Sightsee

Yellowstone National Park

I have never visited it, but I heard that it is beautiful.

Yellowstone National Park is a nearly 3,500-sq.-mile wilderness recreation area atop a volcanic hot spot. Mostly in Wyoming, the park spreads into parts of Montana and Idaho too. Yellowstone features dramatic canyons, alpine rivers, lush forests, hot springs and gushing geysers, including its most famous, Old Faithful. It's also home to hundreds of animal species, including bears, wolves, bison, elk and antelope.

The Bahamas

I went to the Bahamas on a cruise in December of 2019.

The Bahamas, known officially as the Commonwealth of The Bahamas, is a sovereign country within the Lucayan Archipelago of the West Indies in the Atlantic. It takes up 97% of the Lucayan Archipelago's land area and is home to 88% of the archipelago's population.

Disney World

I went to Disney World in July of 2019.

The Walt Disney World Resort, also called Walt Disney World or Disney World, is an entertainment resort complex in Bay Lake and Lake Buena Vista, Florida, United States, near the cities of Orlando and Kissimmee.

The Butterfly Conservatory

I went to the Butterfly Conservatory in December of 2020.

The Niagara Parks Butterfly Conservatory is a butterfly house operated by the Niagara Parks Commission in Niagara Falls, Ontario, Canada. It is located approximately 9 kilometres north of Niagara Falls on the grounds of the Niagara Parks School of Horticulture, which is 40 hectares in size.

Reviews on my Favorite Movies

Frozen II
It was a really good family film, and had tons of comedy for small kids.

Frozen II was a really good movie. My family and I watched it with my neighbor and her family in a 4D theater the day it came out. It was a really cool continuation of Frozen, and the story was built really well. The exposition begins 6 years after Elsa is crowned, which means 3 years after Frozen. It also begins with the sisters being young and a flashback of their dad's. After a song and the title scene, the real movie starts, and it is really sad at some parts. The roles switch, and Anna becmes overprotective of Elsa instaed of the other way around, and they travel to an enchanted forest to face a threat to Arendelle. How they retract the threat, reunite, free th enchanted people, and what they sacrifice forms the rest of the story.

Ballerina
It shows the struggles of an orphan with a big dream, and how she achieves her goal.

Ballerina was one of the best movies that Disney came up with. Felicity is an orphan whose late mother was a dancer (which us revealed much later in the movie). All she has is her best friend, Victor, and he is very supportive of her. Unfortunately, their headmistress is not, and keeps scolding her when she tries to praactice ballet. Finally, Victor and Felicity run away from their orphanage, and after many encounters, they escape and arrive where they needed to go. There, they both separate ways and try to accomplish their different goals. What comes in between them and their goals or what helps them finishes the semi-sentimental movie.

Encanto
This movie is really funny, and it shows how the one different girl ends up saving her entire family and showing the head what the true meaning of the miracle was.

Encanto was the sweetest movie I had ever seen. Mirabel is the black sheep of her family. She is the only one that wasn't blessed witha gift, and she keeps on getting bad luck. She tries to help, but it isn't worth anything to her abuela. Her mom and dad were always extra nice to her, but as the movie progresses, she earns the respect of her entire family, including abuela. She even patches the family up with her estranged Tio Bruno, who left a long time ago to protect her. How she saves her home and family's safety completes the rest of the movie.

The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch, and the Wardrobe
This movie is for adventure lovers. It is also kind of sad, but very well filmed.

The Lion, the Witch, and the Wardrobe is one of 7 books from The Chronicles of Narnia. In my opinion, it is the best out of the 7, as the rest are really boring. It is also the best movie out of the 7, for th exact same reason. The film circles around 4 siblings: Peter, Susan, Edmund, and Lucy. Lucy first discovers Narnia inside the wardrobe in the spare room. Her siblings don't believe her, but then they stumble upon it as well. The adventure they get themselves into, and how they save Narnia from the evil White Witch finishes this slightly bittersweet masterpiece.
